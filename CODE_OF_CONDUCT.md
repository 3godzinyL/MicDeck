# Community code of conduct

MicDeck should be a useful, welcoming project for users and contributors of
every experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while remaining clear about technical evidence.
- Respect privacy, licenses, and content ownership.
- Help newcomers reproduce problems and understand decisions.

Harassment, hate speech, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, spam, and
deliberate disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

Maintainers may edit or remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when needed to protect the community. Serious incidents should
be reported privately through the repository's security reporting channel.
